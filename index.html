My Top 3 Favorite Foods


Cheesesteak

Cheesesteaks definitely make the list for one of my favorite foods. My favorite places to get Cheesesteaks are Charley's Philly Cheesteaks and Burger Brothers.

Sushi

Some specific Sushi rolls I love are spicy tuna rolls, californiarolls, and shrimp tempura rolls. My favorite place to get sushi is Ganga.

Pizza

More specifically, my favorite type of pizza is pepperoni, coal-fired pizza. My favorite place to get pizza is Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza.
